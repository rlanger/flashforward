Basic Training

On your right monitor is a virtual 3D scene that includes a cube, a light, and a camera.

Basic navigation

You can use the following techniques to adjust your view of the virtual scene:

  • Press the middle mouse button and move the mouse to rotate the view
  • Press Shift and the middle mouse button to pan the view
  • Press Control and the middle mouse button to zoom the view

Take a moment to experiment with these controls now.

Selecting objects

Click on the cube with your right mouse button to select it. You will see it framed in an orangey-yellow outline.

Manipulating objects

Press Control + Space to toggle the visibility of the manipulator. The manipulator can be used to changes the position, rotation, and size of an object.

While the manipulator is visible, try the following:

  • Select the position button (), then drag on any of the coloured lines of the manipulator with the left mouse button to move the camera in the corresponding direction.
  • Select the rotation button (), then drag on any of the coloured lines of the manipulator with the left mouse button to move the camera in the corresponding direction.
  • Select the scale button (), then drag on any of the coloured lines of the manipulator with the left mouse button to move the camera in the corresponding direction.
