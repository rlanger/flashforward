Welcome to the Blender Animation tutorial.

Your other screen should look like this:

  1. Fix the values of Right, Left, and Squish to 0.000.
  2. Move the green bar to frame 15. Change the value of Right to 1.000.
  3. Move the green bar to frame 30. Change the value of Right to 0.000 and fix the value Left to 0.000.
  4. Use the right arrow key to move the green bar forward to frame 45.
  5. Change the value of Left to 1.000.
  6. Move the green bar to frame 60.
  7. Change the value of Left to 0.000 and fix the value of Squish at 0.000.
  8. Move the green bar to frame 75.
  9. Change the value of Squish to 1.000.
  10. Move the green bar to frame 90.
  11. Change the value of Squish to 0.000.

Your first animation is now complete! Press Alt-A to watch a preview of the animation. Press ESC to stop the preview.

This is only the beginning! In time, you'll learn to animate many different attributes of a shape, animate complex shapes, and render your animations to make polished 3D animated movies.

First though, you'll have to go back to the very beginning and learn the basics of 3D animation in Blender.
